Ahmedabad

Ahmedabad is the largest city and former capital of Gujarat, which is a state in India. Ahmedabad is located on the banks of the Sabarmati River, 30 km (19 mi) from the state capital Gandhinagar, which is its twin city. In 2010, Ahmedabad was ranked third in Forbes's list of fastest growing cities of the decade. In July 2017, the Historic City of Ahmadabad or Old Ahmadabad, was declared as India's first UNESCO World Heritage City.
