Arvind Kejriwal

Arvind Kejriwal is an Indian politician and a former bureaucrat and Chief Minister of Delhi. He is the national convener of the Aam Aadmi Party. Kejriwal is a graduate of the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, and worked in the Indian Revenue Service as a Joint Commissioner of the Income Tax Department in New Delhi.
