Mumbai

Mumbai also known as Bombay, the official name until 1995 is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is the most populous city in India with an estimated city proper population of 12.4 million as of 2011. It is also the wealthiest city in India,and has the highest number of millionaires and billionaires among all cities in India. The city also houses India's Hindi Bollywood and Marathi cinema industries.
