Hyderabad

Hyderabad is the capital of the Indian state of Telangana and de jure capital of Andhra Pradesh. Hyderabad was historically known as a pearl and diamond trading centre, and it continues to be known as the "City of Pearls". Hyderabad is the fifth-largest contributor to India's overall gross domestic product
