Himalayas

The Himalayas, or Himalaya , form a mountain range in Asia separating the plains of the Indian subcontinent from the Tibetan Plateau.The Himalayan range has many of the Earth's highest peaks, including the highest, Mount Everest. The Himalayas have profoundly shaped the cultures of the Indian subcontinent; many Himalayan peaks are sacred in Hinduism and Buddhism.
